About the Book

"100 Days of Ashes" is a gripping interactive novel that explores the resilience of a nation and its people after a devastating disaster that shook the young nation to its core. You are selected in its wake as the Interim Governor, as to lead the people out of ruins. Will you succeed or will the aftermath face too difficult?
